Report Card Lookup

View your child's released Half-Yearly report card. No login required.

ⓘ How to view your child's report card

  1. Select your child's Class from the list.
  2. Enter their Roll Number.
  3. Enter their Date of Birth exactly as recorded at school.
  4. Tap View Report Card — it opens below, with an option to download or print.

Note: Only released Half-Yearly report cards can be viewed. If you see “no report card found”, please re-check the details or contact the school office.
