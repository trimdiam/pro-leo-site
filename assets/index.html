Our Story

A Legacy of 45+ Years

St. Francis School, Laitkor was established in 1978 by the Franciscan Brothers under the Diocese of Shillong. For over four decades, the school has been a beacon of quality education in Meghalaya, nurturing thousands of young minds who have gone on to serve in every walk of life.

The school is affiliated with the Meghalaya Board of School Education (MBOSE) and follows the national curriculum, offering classes from Kindergarten to Grade 10. With a strong emphasis on both academic excellence and character formation, St. Francis School stands as one of the premier educational institutions in the region.

A Word From

Principal's Message

Fr. Thomas Xavier
Principal

"Dear Parents, Students, and Well-Wishers,

It is with great joy and a deep sense of responsibility that I address you as the Principal of St. Francis School, Laitkor. Education, as we believe it, is not merely the transfer of knowledge — it is the transformation of a human soul.

Our motto — 'Learning to know, to grow, to love, to care and to serve' — encapsulates everything we strive for. We do not only prepare students for examinations; we prepare them for life. We want our students to be kind, honest, compassionate, and responsible citizens of tomorrow.

I invite every parent to partner with us in this noble mission. Your involvement, encouragement at home, and trust in our faculty make a world of difference in your child's journey.

Together, let us build a community of learners who are not only brilliant in mind but rich in heart.

God bless you all.

Fr. Thomas Xavier, SJ
Principal, St. Francis School

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that empowers every student with knowledge, values, and skills to become compassionate, responsible, and contributing members of society — inspired by the spirit of St. Francis of Assisi.

Our Mission

To provide holistic, quality education rooted in Christian values that nurtures intellectual growth, spiritual awareness, physical development, and social responsibility — making each student a life-long learner and servant leader.

Our Core Values

Knowledge

We believe in the transformative power of learning. Education opens doors and shapes destinies.

Growth

Every child has unique potential. We nurture it with patience, creativity, and encouragement.

Love

Inspired by St. Francis, we build a community of unconditional love and mutual respect.

Care

We care for the whole person — mind, body, and spirit — leaving no child behind.

Service

True education leads to service. We instil a spirit of giving back to society and community.
